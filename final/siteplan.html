Hazard Research Labs

Purpose

Hazard Research Labs are a government contracted organization of independent teams of scientists working just outside of Hazardville, Delaware. This website is designed to inform people about what takes place at Hazard Research Labs, as well as allowing people to sign up to participate in various research projects that take place at the labs.

Target Audience

Hazard Research Labs has recently had trouble finding... willing participants for their Government Approved research projects. As such, they have been awarded a grant to develop a website in order to encourage people to learn more about what goes on behind closed lab doors. The website is intended to be somewhere for people to learn about the experiments, as well as apply to be part of them. The grant also includes means to provide monetary incentive to participants, as previous compensation proved to be insufficient to retain quality test subjects.
