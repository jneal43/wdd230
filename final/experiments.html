Hazard Research Labs

Experiments

Here is a collection of some of the experiments we have been running. Right now we are focused on researching different colored liquids. Test subjects have been helping us understand the difference between the different liquids and determine what applications they may have in the real world. Progress has been slow going, however, so we hope incorporating liquids into most of the testing for the forseeable future will help us understand more about them.
