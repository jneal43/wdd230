Join our Research Program!

What's in it for you?

Compensation is one of the first things on people's minds when they think about signing up for Experiments with Hazard Research Labs. Whether you're looking for a quick round of tests or want to really experience all of what Hazard Research Labs has to offer, there's sure to be an option that suits your needs.

Single Day Experiments

Got some free time? Why not spend it getting experimented on down at Hazard Research Labs! A single afternoon pays $50, but if you get here early enough to do two sessions we'll pay you $100 for your time.

Weekend Long Experiments

If you are looking to get away for a bit longer than a single evening we have weekend long options that begin Friday evening and conclude Sunday afternoon. Expect to receive $200 for your time.

Extended Stay Research Opportunities

Are you looking for something more permanent? Apply to be a part of our Extended Stay Research Opportunity program today!

Group Research Opportunities

Got some friends with nothing to do? Bring up to 10 people and participate in our Group Research Opportunity.
