Hazard Research Labs

Group Research Opportunities

Discover new things about your friends and family with Hazard Research Labs Group Research Opportunities! While we do not have the means to support long term groups, we would love to see you and up to 9 other people for an afternoon at a time.

Our Group Research Opportunities are a great way to bring people together while helping advance the field of Science. Bring your kids or your coworkers and strengthen your bonds during a round or two of testing and experimentation. Each participant over the age of 18 will receive $50 as compensation for their time. While we can't legally pay anyone under the age of 18, each of age participant will receive $50 per dependent per test as well.
